COVID-19 By Week

All data are updated daily, about 5:15am UTC, including any historical corrections. Weeks are labeled by their final, anchored to today.

Series w/ "Rate" in their name are per day per 100K population.

COVID-19 and population data from the COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University.
