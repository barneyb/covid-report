US COVID-19 by Jurisdiction

Population rates are calculated per 100,000 people.

Time rates are calculated per week (7 days).

Dates are the CDC's publishing dates, typically the day after the data are reported.

If data are available, a bad-to-good color gradient will be laid behind normalized columns to indicate the relative change from the prior week.

COVID-19 case and death data from https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/cases-updates/cases-in-us.html.

Population data from https://www.census.gov/data/datasets/time-series/demo/popest/2010s-state-total.html.
