Worldwide COVID-19 by Country

Starting July 6, tables show average daily rates for the week, instead of a weekly rate, for easier comparison with the dashboard and charts.

All data are updated daily, about 5:15am UTC, including any historical corrections. Weeks are labeled by their last day.

Series w/ "Rate" in their name are per day per 100K population.

You can download the raw table as table-ww.tsv.

COVID-19 and population data from COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University.
