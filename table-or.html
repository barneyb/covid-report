Oregon COVID-19 by County

All data are updated daily, just after 4am UTC, including any historical corrections. Weeks are labeled by their last day.

Series w/ "Rate" in their name are per week per 100K population.

The Portland metro (tri-county) area is home to nearly half of Oregonians. Additional total rows are provided along this partition.

You can download the raw tables as table-or.tsv.

COVID-19 and population data from COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University.
