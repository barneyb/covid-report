US COVID-19 by State

All data are updated daily, just after 4am UTC, including any historical corrections. Weeks are labeled by their last day.

Series w/ "Rate" or "DR" in their name are per week per 100K population.

New York's and New Jersey's disproportionate case load makes the total row difficult to interpret as a "national average". Additional total rows are provided with these jurisdictions excluded.

You can download the raw tables as table-us.tsv and mortality.csv.

COVID-19 and population data from COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University.

Mortality data from https://wonder.cdc.gov/controller/datarequest/D140 using year 2016.
