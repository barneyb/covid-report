Deaths per Day per 100,000 Population (7-day Rolling Average)

All data are updated daily, just after 4am UTC, including both the prior day's data and any historical corrections.

New York's disproportionate case load makes the US series difficult to interpret as a "national average" from mid-March to mid-May. A "US Except NY" series is provided with New York excluded.

You can download the raw tables as rates-deaths.txt and events.txt.

COVID-19 and population data from COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University.

Event data from various media and government sources.
